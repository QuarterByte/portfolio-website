Projectile Launcher (2023)

Arduino Uno, 3D Design and Printing, C++, Circuits

I made a projectile launcher that is capable of launching a projectile up to five feet forwards and two feet upwards with an adjustable angle that is controlled by an Arduino Uno. The stepper motor used was powered by an external power supply.

The angle is accurate to half a degree, though could have been made more accurate with more precise hardware. When tested, the projectile launched using the launcher landed within a few inches where predicted, which was remarkably accurate.

The launcher itself is 3D printed, and all of the files used were made in Fusion 360.

Locker Camera (2023)

Raspberry Pi 3B+, Python, Pip, Image Encoding, Electronics

I made this locker camera to document my senior year. The camera is an Arducam 5 attached to a Raspberry Pi. A battery bank inside the locker powered the Pi.

The Pi would start recording whenever the PIR sensor picked up motion (when the locker was opened). The Pi would stop and save the recording when the locker was closed. The recordings would get saved and exported off of the SD card later since the Pi's GPU was slower at encoding than my computer.

Overall, the project taught me a lot about electronics and their behaviour, alongside video encoding and the computing power of microcontrollers.

Ethereum Mining Rig (2022)

Linux (HiveOS), Computer Assembly, BIOS optimization, Networking

In 2022, I built a multi-GPU rig setup for validating transactions on the Ethereum blockchain (mining).

The rig primarily used GPUs to perform mathematical processes to validate transactions on the Ethereum network. For each validated transactions done by the rig, the network would pay the rig a small fee in return.

The computer rig was carefully overclocked to ensure best performance while maintaining the longevity of the components.

The rig was configured on the network to be accessible remotely for features such as remote reset and changing performance settings, such as the fan speeds and overclock settings.
